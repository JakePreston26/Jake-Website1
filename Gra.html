Graphic Communications

The project that we worked on was Humans of DVC, which is based on the "Humans of New York" photography piece. We were tasked with taking a picture of a "human of DVC" (one of our peers) in order to capture their personality. We also had to write a short story using there words that gave insight into who they are as a person. This project taught us how to capture the essence of the subject in each photo. Reflection

I really enjoyed interviewing my subject because I feel like I got to learn a lot about her as a person. I learned a lot about her experiences through the answers she gave to my questions. I really enjoyed the interview process because I feel like interviewing came easy to me. Some advice I would give to anyone doing this project is:

  • Make sure to personally get to know your subject
  • Make sure the picture captures the essence of your subject
  • Make sure that your story relates heavily to the emotion that your picture evokes
