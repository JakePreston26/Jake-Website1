The project that we are working on is the "What we keep" project. For it we have to write a short story about an item that is very important and special to us. We chose an item that represented our past, present, and future. Each item should encapsulate each time period of our lives. Each picture below represents a period of my life, and by clicking on the picture it will take you to the story that I wrote to encapsulate how each object affects my life.

I enjoyed making this project because I enjoyed reliving some moments from my past through the items that I chose. I also enjoyed thinking about my future through the object i chose for the future. I feel like the project could have been improved by:

  1. Giving us better guidelines as to what to write about.
  2. Giving us examples of how a student could have done it in the past.
  3. Allowing us to recieve more feedback from our teacher or peers.
